# ZapArc – App Store Description

## Short Tagline
Self-custodial Bitcoin & Lightning wallet with fast, private payments.

## Full Description
ZapArc is a privacy-first, self-custodial Bitcoin and Lightning wallet built for everyday use. You control your keys, your funds, and your data—no accounts, no KYC, and no tracking. Send and receive Bitcoin in seconds with Lightning, or use on-chain Bitcoin when you need it.

Designed for speed and simplicity, ZapArc keeps everything on your device and never collects personal information. Your recovery phrase stays with you, and you’re always in control.

## Key Features
- **Self-custodial**: You hold the keys—only you control your funds.
- **Lightning fast payments**: Send and receive sats instantly.
- **No KYC / No accounts**: Start using the wallet immediately.
- **Privacy-focused**: No analytics, no tracking, no data collection.
- **On-device security**: Wallet data stays local to your device.
- **Open source (if applicable)**: Verifiable, transparent code.
- **Simple UX**: Clean, intuitive interface for everyday Bitcoin use.
