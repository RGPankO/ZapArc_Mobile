# ZapArc Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-02-10

ZapArc is a self-custodial Bitcoin and Lightning wallet. We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains what data we do (and do not) collect.

## Summary
- **No accounts or registration** required.
- **No data collected or transmitted to our servers.**
- **Wallet data is stored locally on your device only.**
- **Recovery phrase is your responsibility** and is never transmitted off-device.
- **No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.**
- **Open source** (if applicable to your build/distribution).

## Data We Do Not Collect
We do not collect, store, or transmit:
- Names, emails, phone numbers, or identifiers
- IP addresses or device identifiers
- Transaction history or balances
- Location data
- Usage analytics or behavioral data

## Data Stored on Your Device
ZapArc stores wallet data locally on your device, including:
- Wallet keys and Lightning channel data
- Transaction history
- App preferences

This data remains on your device unless you choose to back it up externally.

## Recovery Phrase
Your recovery phrase (seed) is generated and stored on your device. **You are solely responsible for securing it.** If you lose your recovery phrase, we cannot restore your wallet or funds.

## Third-Party Services
ZapArc does not integrate analytics or tracking services. Network operations are limited to interacting with the Bitcoin and Lightning networks necessary for wallet functionality.

## Open Source
If the ZapArc build you are using is open source, the source code is publicly available. You can review it to verify our privacy practices.

## Children’s Privacy
ZapArc is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or in the app’s documentation.
