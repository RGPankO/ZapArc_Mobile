In short: ZapArc does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data stays on your device.

1. Overview

ZapArc is a self-custodial Bitcoin Lightning wallet. The app is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not operate servers that collect your data, and we do not have access to your wallet, keys, or transaction history.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using encrypted secure storage:

  • Wallet keys and seed phrases
  • PIN and security settings
  • Transaction history
  • Address book contacts
  • App preferences (language, currency, theme)

None of this data is transmitted to our servers. We do not have servers that receive user data.

3. Device Permissions

Camera: Used solely to scan QR codes for Lightning payments. Camera images are processed locally and are never stored or transmitted.

Location: May be used to suggest local currency settings. Location data is processed on-device only and is never sent externally.

Notifications: Used for local payment and wallet notifications. No personal data is collected through the notification system.

4. Third-Party Services

Breez SDK: ZapArc uses the Breez SDK to connect to the Bitcoin Lightning Network. When you send or receive payments, transaction data is processed through Lightning Network nodes as required by the protocol. Please refer to the Breez SDK Privacy Policy for details.

5. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No email addresses or passwords
  • No personal identification information
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No device identifiers
  • No advertising data

6. Data Security

Your wallet data is encrypted on your device using your PIN. If you uninstall the app or lose your device without a backup, your wallet data cannot be recovered by us or anyone else. You are responsible for backing up your seed phrase.

7. Children's Privacy

ZapArc is not directed at children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app's functionality changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
