Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 8, 2026

ZapArc ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password (stored securely as a hash). If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from Google.

Camera Access: The app requests camera permission solely to scan QR codes. Images from the camera are processed locally on your device and are not stored or transmitted.

Location Data: The app may request your location to suggest local currency and region settings. Location data is used only on your device and is not sent to our servers.

Push Notifications: We use Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver push notifications. A device token is stored to enable this service.

Payment Information: The app supports Bitcoin Lightning payments via the Breez SDK. Payment transactions are processed on the Lightning Network. We do not collect or store your wallet keys or payment details on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • To send push notifications you have opted into
  • To process payments you initiate
  • To improve the app experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, push notifications, and analytics
  • Breez SDK: For processing Lightning Network payments

These services have their own privacy policies governing data use.

4. Data Storage and Security

Account data is stored on secure servers. Sensitive credentials are stored locally on your device using encrypted secure storage. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

5. Your Rights

  • Access & Delete: You can view your profile data in the app and delete your account at any time from the settings screen.
  • Permissions: You can revoke camera and location permissions at any time through your device settings.
  • Notifications: You can disable push notifications through your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
