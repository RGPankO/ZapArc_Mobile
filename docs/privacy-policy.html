ZapArc Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 10, 2026

In short: ZapArc does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data. All wallet data stays locally on your device, and you control your keys.

1. No Data Collection

ZapArc is a self-custodial Bitcoin Lightning wallet. We do not collect personal information, analytics, or device identifiers. We do not run servers that receive your data.

2. Local Storage Only

All wallet data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Wallet keys and seed phrases
  • Transaction history
  • App settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device.

3. You Control Your Keys

ZapArc is self-custodial. You control your private keys and funds. We do not have access to your wallet, keys, or transaction history.

4. No Accounts, No Tracking

ZapArc does not require user accounts and does not track your activity. There is no advertising or third-party tracking.

5. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
